Why the NFL Is King

The National Football League (NFL) is the most popular and profitable sports league in the United States. With 32 franchises split between the AFC and NFC, the NFL showcases the highest level of American football in the world.

Each season culminates in the Super Bowl, one of the most watched sporting events globally. The league blends elite athleticism, strategy, physicality, and spectacle.
